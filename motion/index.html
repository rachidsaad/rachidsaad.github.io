Introduction

Get ready for an immersive and thought-provoking experience that explores the synergy between the body and cultural expression. Taking place on Thursday, February 27, and Friday, February 28, 2025, the festival offers a diverse array of conferences, live performances, hands-on workshops, and sporting activities.

Bringing together Moroccan and international artists and scholars, the festival seeks to deconstruct the body, not merely as a biological entity of flesh and bone, but as a fluid vessel shaped by cultural narratives and representations.

Join us in this celebration of body, culture, and motion, where creativity and scholarly inquiry converge to bring forth new insights into human expression and identity.
