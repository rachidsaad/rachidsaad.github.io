Call for Papers

The Concept

Dedicated to the figure of the mother, this edition explores movement, transformation, and the crossing of spaces, times, and norms. We view the maternal body as a living, mobile, and political entity.


In a context of contemporary change—geographical mobility, transformations in family models, and feminist struggles—the mother's body appears as a space of resistance and reinvention.

The Vision

A space where academic research, artistic creation, and bodily practices converge to rethink motherhood beyond traditional assignments.


  • Scientific Presentations
  • Performances & Dance
  • Art Installations
  • Workshops & Round Tables

Areas of Exploration

Transformations

Pregnancy, postpartum, and aging as biological and identity-shifting movements.

Arts & Media

Representations in literature, visual arts, cinema, and contemporary dance.

Resistance

The political body, medicalization, social norms, and feminist critiques.
