The Game Awards 2019 nominees unveiled

The Game Awards are back this year, and they announced their nominee list this week. Super Smash Bros. Ultimate was nominated for Game of the Year along with Death Stranding, Resident Evil 2, Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice, Control, and The Outer Worlds.

All Nintendo games nominated:

  • Game of the Year: Super Smash Bros. Ultimate
  • Action Game: Astral Chain
  • Action/Adventure: The Legend of Zelda: Link’s Awakening
  • Art Direction: The Legend of Zelda: Link’s Awakening
  • Family Game: Luigi’s Mansion 3, Ring Fit Adventure, Super Mario Maker 2, Super Smash Bros. Ultimate, and Yoshi’s Crafted World
  • Fighting Game: Super Smash Bros. Ultimate
  • Multiplayer Game: Tetris 99
  • Score & Music: Cadence of Hyrule
  • Strategy Game: Fire Emblem: Three Houses

After last year’s announcement of Joker in Super Smash Bros. Ultimate, there’s speculation running amuck that Fighter #5 will be shown at The Game Awards. Sakurai has put out a tweet about the event. A translation of this tweet was published by PushDustIn, founder and former owner of SourceGaming.
